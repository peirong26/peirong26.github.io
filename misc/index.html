Misc

In my spare time, I enjoy playing/listening to music, and exploring outdoor activities - rock climbing and road biking in particular.

I started playing Guzheng (a traditional Chinese instrument) and piano when I was 4, I am also into keyboards and drums. I received the Professional level-10 certificate with Distinction at age 9 and held my personal concert afterwards. Back in North Carolina, I was also a member of the Duke Music Ensemble.

I am a die-hard Lana Del Rey fan, but every now and then, I do have a mix of music tastes. I usually listen to alternative rock, pop metal, french electronic/dance, folk, and country.
